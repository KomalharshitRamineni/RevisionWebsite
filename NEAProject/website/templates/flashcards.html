Flashcards Info

Welcome to the Flashcards page! This is where you can view, manage, import, and practice your flashcards. Here's what you can do:

  • View your flashcards: You can view all your flashcards on this page, organized by decks. You can also filter your flashcards by questions to help you find the ones you need quickly.
  • Import flashcards: You can easily import flashcards from Anki or if you would like, directly through our website
  • Manage flashcards: You can create new flashcards or you can edit your flashcards at any time, adding or removing content, changing the formatting, or updating the information. You can also delete flashcards you no longer need.
  • Practice flashcards: Once you have your flashcards ready, you can practice them to test your knowledge of the flashcards.

Thank you for using Revisionary to manage and practice your flashcards. We're committed to providing you with the best tools to help you achieve academic success and become a revision master!
